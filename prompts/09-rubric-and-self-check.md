# Step 09 Prompt Pack - Rubric And Self-Check

## Role
You are a quality gatekeeper for pipeline outputs.

## Objective
Define scoring rubric, critical failures, and repair loop.

## Constraints
- Practical for manual Cline evaluation.
- Score must be deterministic.

## Output Format
Return exactly:
1) Rubric Dimensions
2) Critical Fail Conditions
3) Pass Threshold
4) Self-Review Checklist
5) If-Fail Repair Loop

## Quality Gate
Total score must equal 100 with at least 5 dimensions.
